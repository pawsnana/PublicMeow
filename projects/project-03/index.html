Over the past decade, Riot Games has introduced a variety of music groups, each featuring a distinct genre. These groups are composed of in-game Champions in League of Legends, each with unique lore and stories. Dive into the musical universe by exploring this interactive map! Collect three members from a group to unlock their iconic music video and experience their signature song!

Akali

True Damage

Rapper



Akali, the dynamic ninja rapper from K/DA, voiced by (G)i-dle's Soyeon, captivates again with her neon outfit and striking performance. Her scene-stealing rap, delivered in Korean, shines under neon lights, quickly endearing her to fans and highlighting her as a central figure of K/DA's show.

Ekko

True Damage

Rapper



Ekko, traditionally an inventive kid from League of Legends' gritty Zaun city, truly stands out in True Damage's show. His portrayal by Duckwrth and Thutmose, both renowned for their work on the "Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse" soundtrack, adds a dynamic edge to his character, elevating his presence.

Senna

True Damage

Lead Vocalist



Senna, a character who defied her fate in a demon's lantern, is voiced by Keke Palmer. In the lore, once trapped by Thresh in a lantern and haunted by the Black Mist, she emerged transformed, mastering both darkness and light. Now, she combats the Mist with her relic weapon, liberating lost souls with each blast.

Qiyana

True Damage

Lead Vocalist



Qiyana, the elemental assassin with a ring blade, comes to life through Latin pop star Becky G's voice. Sporting a special Louis Vuitton outfit, a unique collaboration between Riot Games and the luxury brand, she dazzles in the video. This exclusive design is also available as an in-game skin for players.

Yasuo

True Damage

DJ



Yasuo, traditionally a wind-commanding samurai, takes on the role of the group's DJ, silently contributing to the ensemble. Deeply principled and skilled, this Ionian swordsman faced turmoil, being wrongly accused of his master's murder and tragically confronting his own brother. Despite finding truth and redemption, he continues his journey, guided only by the wind.

Morgana

Star Guardian



A mysterious villainess who claims to be an envoy from dark space, Morgana sends monsters rampaging through Valoran City in an attempt to force Akali to fall, fragmenting the Star Guardians and allowing her to destroy them one by one. Convinced that justice and goodness are immutable, single-minded ideas that don’t properly respect the darkness of the universe, she seeks to snuff them out entirely... along with anyone who gets in her way.

Syndra

Star Guardian



Feared and admired, Syndra is a formidable Star Guardian from an earlier era. Like Janna, her history is cloaked in shadow, but it’s clear as day that she is an ambitious warrior who is quick to dismiss those she deems unworthy of her time. Currently, she acts as an advisor on Ahri’s team, guiding them away from unforeseen dangers and toward a promising future. Despite Ahri’s trust in her, some members have questioned the veteran’s true motives and suspect there is no price that Syndra won’t pay to achieve her personal goals.

Zoe

Star Guardian



A member of the ancient, first group of Star Guardians, Zoe’s heart desired not love, but chaos - twisting her newfound power into a malignant expression of cosmic madness. After numberless eons of hunting the First Star’s chosen, she has finally come to Valoran City, hoping to destroy the latest generation of Guardians as she has countless others.

Lulu

Star Guardian



The youngest member of Lux’s team, Lulu is an odd but gifted second-year at Valoran High School. Her unique relationship with the Starlight, the source of every Star Guardian’s power, lets her communicate with it like a close companion. To Lulu, this inner world is the most wondrous place, and anything outside of it is not as interesting. Because of her fascination with the Starlight, outwardly she may appear unconcerned with the gravity of her responsibilities. This whimsical behavior is puzzling to some teammates, but they accept her and all her eccentricities.

Lux

Star Guardian



Cheerful, courageous, and just a bit clumsy, Lux shines the brightest among her Star Guardian team as its captain. While young and inexperienced compared to teammates like Janna, the pink-haired teen’s optimism inspires others in the face of an unkind—but in her eyes, noble—destiny. Protecting the universe is a responsibility that Lux is committed to, even if she doesn’t fully realize the gravity of it just yet. There are many challenges ahead on her journey to becoming a strong leader worthy of the First Star’s call. For now, Lux truly believes that as long as they fight together, there is no darkness they can’t overcome.

Xayah

Star Guardian



Abandoned by her captain and teammates, Xayah spent years in darkness. She sought revenge—yet deep down, she only wanted an explanation, an answer as to why Ahri left Rakan and her to die. Xayah pursued her former friends across the galaxy until, during the Battle of the Twilight Star, Rakan sacrificed his own light to purge the darkness in Xayah's soul. Now a Star Guardian once more, Xayah searches the night skies for her love. She hopes to return the favor and bring him back to the light, but to do so, she must battle the darkness that still rages inside. Xayah's love is her guiding force—though she'd never admit to something so horribly sentimental.

Ahri

Star Guardian



Ahri is a charismatic captain who leads a team of both new and veteran Star Guardians, from the outer edges of the cosmos. Having experienced the pain of losing teammates in the line of duty, she is viciously protective of her “family,” and a strong desire burns within her to ensure no one she cares for ever fades away again. These events have also shaped how she approaches other teams and their captains, especially the less experienced ones like Lux. There’s no more room for patience and understanding in her universe—tough love is all that’s left. Though that be the case, none are immune to Ahri’s own unique charms.

Akali

Star Guardian



A street-smart, gregarious city girl with an upbeat attitude, Akali's life is turned upside-down by the appearance of the Star Guardians... and the destruction that follows them. Taking the oath alongside her best friend Kai'Sa, she stands against the enemies of the First Star with finely honed reflexes and an array of powerful Guardian weapons. Can friendship drown out the doubt in her heart, or will Akali be the first of the new generation to fall?

Ezreal

Heartsteel

Vocalist



As a daring adventurer with an innate gift for magic, Ezreal explores ancient catacombs and defies curses with unmatched courage. He relies on his Shuriman gauntlet to unleash potent arcane power, making him a trouble magnet wherever he goes.

Kayn

Heartsteel

Rapper & Instrumentalist



A master of deadly shadow magic, Kayn aspires to lead the Order of Shadow to Ionian supremacy. He wields the sentient weapon Rhaast, grappling with its corrupting influence. Either he controls it or faces the doom of Runeterra.

Sett

Heartsteel

Co-leader & Rapper



Rising through Ionia's criminal underworld after the Noxian war, Sett gained notoriety for his savage strength and resilience. From humble beginnings in Navori's fighting pits, he now reigns over them as a formidable leader. His indomitable spirit and combat prowess make him an influential figure in the underworld.

Yone

Heartsteel

Producer



Once Yone, now cursed by a malevolent entity, he relentlessly hunts these spirits. Forced to wear a demonic mask, he seeks understanding in life's relentless battles.

K'Sante

Heartsteel

Co-leader & Vocalist



Fierce and noble, K'Sante defends Nazumah against colossal threats. A falling-out with his partner leads him on a path to self-improvement. As a co-leader, he strives to protect his oasis and learn from past mistakes.

Akali

K/DA

Rapper



Akali makes appearances next to other street performers in cities she is visiting. Combining mixed martial arts and the beat of her own rap lyrics, she delights audiences with her bold lyrical rap and punk ninja style. She started practicing on the streets before rising to fame and returns to her roots whenever she can. Her unruly hair and untamed style made Akali an instant K/DA fan favorite.

Ahri

K/DA

Lead Vocalist



After rising to fame as a teenage pop star, Ahri tossed aside her girly and young look to reveal her new self: a high fashion, elegant, and stunning celebrity. Ahri’s sleek new look attracts top fashion designers. During fashion week, Ahri graces runways around the world in finale gowns. She is the face of FOXY cosmetics and launched her own fragrance, Charmed last year. When she isn’t with K/DA or training, Ahri is shopping, drinking tea with designers, and testing out new beauty products.

Kai'Sa

K/DA

Lead Dancer



Kai'Sa, known as the lead dancer of K/DA, dances to express a deep, inner symphony of movement. Her dance is a response to an almost mystical call, a blend of powerful emotions and a desire to tell a story through motion. She sees dance as a journey from the void to the vibrancy of life, weaving narratives with every step and movement.

Evelynn

K/DA

Lead Vocalist



Eve’s reputation paints her as a demanding diva. She told Pop Shine, “I’m an artist, not a socialite. I won’t apologize for high standards.” Eve once walked off the stage of a live performance when her vocals were backed by an audio track she didn’t approve. Though her presence in the industry is turbulent, she has a diehard fan base who call themselves “Deeva.” Prior to K/DA, Evelynn released two hit singles, Agony’s Embrace and Ecstasy.

Olaf

Pentakill

Drummer



Good drummers possess rhythm, a staggering body count, and an insatiable thirst for battle. Olaf, PENTAKILL's exceptional drummer, embodies these qualities as a cursed Viking with an insatiable lust for destruction.

Sona

Pentakill

Keyboardist



Sona of PENTAKILL rejects the notion that keyboards can't be metal. Her instrument is a conduit of living barbed wire and a sonic scream that can shatter lesser musical genres, bringing them to their knees in awe of her metallic prowess.

Kayle

Pentakill

Vocalist



KWhen PENTAKILL sought a female vocalist, an angel of rock descended from the heavens. Armed with a sword-microphone, Kayle embodies eternal glory in the realm of heavy metal.

Karthus

Pentakill

Lead Singer



Karthus, the lead singer of heavy-metal sensation PENTAKILL, channels the voice of a fallen angel made of gleaming chrome and explosive dynamite. His vocals ignite the very sun, setting the metal hearts of all who listen ablaze with passion and power.
